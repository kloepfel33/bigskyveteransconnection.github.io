Big Sky Veterans Connection

Montana veterans supporting one another so that no one has to feel alone — no matter when or where they served.

Our Mission

Big Sky Veterans Connection exists to reduce isolation and bring Montana veterans together through connection, peer support, and shared understanding. Whether you are newly separated, decades removed from service, or anywhere in between, this is a place to reconnect with people who understand the transition.

Why This Matters

Transition Is Hard

Leaving military service often means losing structure, daily camaraderie, and a sense of purpose. Many veterans don’t realize how isolating that can feel until it happens.

Small-Town Isolation

In small communities like Butte and across rural Montana, veterans can feel especially disconnected, even when surrounded by good people.

Connection Saves Lives

Having someone to talk to — another veteran who understands without explanation — can make a real difference.

What We Do

  • Provide a judgment-free space for veteran connection
  • Encourage peer-to-peer check-ins and mentorship
  • Share Montana-specific veteran resources
  • Organize informal meetups, coffee chats, and outdoor activities
  • Support veterans who may not feel comfortable in traditional organizations

You Are Not Alone

If you’re a Montana veteran looking for connection, support, or just a place to belong — we’d be glad to have you.
